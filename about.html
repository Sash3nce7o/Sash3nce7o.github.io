About Shia LaBeouf

Shia Saide LaBeouf was born on June 11, 1986, in Los Angeles, California. Known for his versatility, Shia's career spans across acting, directing, and experimental performance art. From starring in blockbuster hits like "Transformers" to creating profound personal projects such as "Honey Boy," Shia continues to challenge the boundaries of art and entertainment.
